## Technical Orchestration

### Music & Audio
- **0-15s**: Low-pass filtered atmospheric pads. Slow, tribal percussion pulse builds.
- **15-50s**: Full orchestral-hybrid score. Deep cinematic hits on "Faith Nexus" and "Covenant Conversations."
- **50-75s**: Uplifting, fast-paced rhythmic finish. High energy.
- **Voice-Over**: Clinical, visionary, and strategic. Pan-African accent with global gravitas.

## Strategic Hook: "Digital Genesis"
The video is designed to drive traffic specifically to the **March 27 Virtual Activation**, using it as the funnel for the physical October summit. The Mascots (Nexus Leader and Nexus Creator) serve as the "hosts" of the video, appearing in key composition nodes.
