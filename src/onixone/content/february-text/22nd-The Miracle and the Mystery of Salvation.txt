The Parchments of Truth by Pastor Alex 22 February 2026Bible: Titus 3Key Verse: 3For we ourselves also were sometimes foolish, disobedient, deceived, serving divers lusts and pleasures, living in malice and envy, hateful, and hating one another.
Theme: The Miracle and the Mystery of Salvation
Message When you look at your life—who you used to be and where God took you from—you cannot deny that salvation is both a miracle and a mystery. It is pure evidence that God is real. If God had not saved you, what would have become of you? This was Paul’s contemplation in 1 Timothy 1:15 when he said, “This is a faithful saying, and worthy of all acceptation, that Christ Jesus came into the world to save sinners; of whom I am chief.” He came specifically for you, just to save you.
Jesus said in Luke 7:47 that to whom much is forgiven, much love is shown. The cost of your forgiveness was the very life of Christ. He died to save humanity. He paid the debt He did not owe—the debt we owed but could not pay. Therefore, do not forget the Lord. Do not allow the worries of this life, the deceitfulness of wealth, or the desire for other things to make you forget where God brought you from.
Life is short—live fully for Him. Let your gratitude be evident through your love for Him, your love for others, and your heartfelt desire to please Him. In return, He will perfect all that concerns you.
Once, He came to die, but again, He is coming as King and righteous Judge of the whole world. The time is short—live fully for Him!
Prayers Glorious Father, I am grateful for Your love toward me and all humanity. You saved us by grace—the grace through Christ Jesus. I declare today that I love You and live to please You. Thank You for perfecting all that concerns me, in Jesus’ name—Amen!
