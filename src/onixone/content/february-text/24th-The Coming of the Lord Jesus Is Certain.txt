The Parchments of Truth by Pastor Alex Iheme 24 February 2026Bible: 2 Peter 3Key Verse: 8But do not forget this one thing, dear friends: With the Lord a day is like a thousand years, and a thousand years are like a day.
Theme: The Coming of the Lord Jesus Is Certain
Message There are those who argue, question, take lightly, and even deny the second coming of Jesus. Concerning these, St. Peter warns: “Above all, you must understand that in the last days scoffers will come, scoffing and following their own evil desires. They will say, ‘Where is this coming he promised? Ever since our ancestors died, everything goes on as it has since the beginning of creation.’” (2 Peter 3:4-5).
Beloved, do not be influenced by men’s ideology of God. Keep your eyes on Jesus and your heart stayed on His word. God is not a man that He should lie; He does not make empty promises. Jesus said, “Heaven and earth will pass away, but my words will never pass away. But about that day or hour no one knows, not even the angels in heaven, nor the Son, but only the Father.” (Matthew 24:35-36).
The patience of God means salvation. In His love, demonstrated through the sacrifice of His only Son, He does not want anyone to perish. God desires that all humanity be saved.
This world is not your home—you are on a pilgrimage. Your mission is to join forces with God to ensure that the gospel is preached to all creatures in all the earth, as a testimony and opportunity for salvation. Then the end will come. Let everything you are and everything you have be a tool for this purpose.
On that day, there will be no excuse. Our present time on earth is but a fragment of seconds in preparation for eternity. Be found faithful—doing the work of the Lord, walking in love, growing in grace, and in the knowledge of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ. Amen!
Prayer Glorious Father, my eyes are fixed on You and my heart is set on Your word. The work of salvation shall prosper in me and through me, in Jesus’ name—Amen!
