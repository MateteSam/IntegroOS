The Parchments of Truth by Pastor Alex 20 February 2026Bible: Hebrews 7Key Verse: 17For it is attested [by God] of him, “You (Christ) are a priest forever according to the order of Melchizedek.” (AMP)
Theme: King of Righteousness and Peace
Message The theme verse is God’s testimony concerning Jesus Christ our Lord—He is the only King that abides forever. Melchizedek by interpretation means “King of Righteousness” and also “King of Peace.” Jesus is both—the King of righteousness and the King of peace. The Bible calls Him the righteous King (Isaiah 32:1) and the Prince of Peace (Isaiah 9:6). Everything that Jesus is, we have become in Him, for “as He is, so are we in this world” (1 John 4:17).
As a believer in Jesus Christ, you are a king of righteousness. God made Jesus, who had no sin, to be sin for us, so that in Him we might become the righteousness of God (2 Corinthians 5:21). You are not just righteous—you are a king of it. Live right before God; it is your nature. Influence others with your righteousness, and they will marvel and seek your God.
You are also a king of peace. You do not only have peace—you are a peacemaker and a peace-giver; it is your nature. Jesus said, “Blessed are the peacemakers, for they will be called children of God” (Matthew 5:9). Peace is the greatest outcome of righteousness and the channel through which wisdom and power proceed (Isaiah 32:17).
Every wisdom from above is pure and peaceable (James 3:17). Carry your peace wherever you go and in whatever you do, and you will become a place of refuge and strength to many.
Confession I am a king of righteousness and a king of peace. My life is an influencer unto righteousness and peace. Blessed be God! Amen.
