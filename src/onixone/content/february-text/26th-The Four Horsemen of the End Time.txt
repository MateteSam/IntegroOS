The Parchments of Truth by Pastor Alex 26 February 2026Bible: Revelation 6Key Verse: 15-17
Theme: The Four Horsemen of the End Time
Lessons Revelation chapter six reveals expressly the events of the last days known as the great tribulation. The first four seals opened by Jesus are referred to as the Four Horsemen of the Apocalypse. These horsemen are symbolic, as the events were brought about through the horses and their riders (Revelation 6:1-7).
The First Seal: A white horse and its rider, representing the Antichrist, who comes with intentions to conquer and rule the earth through great deception.
The Second Seal: A red horse and its rider, who takes peace from the earth, causing men to kill one another with swords.
The Third Seal: A black horse and its rider, symbolizing great famine on the earth.
The Fourth Seal: A pale horse and its rider, whose name is Death, with Hell following close behind. With great power, they amplify tribulation and suffering through war, hunger, and widespread death.
These revelations are not new in the Bible. Jesus already foretold them when He spoke of the end-time signs. In Matthew 24:5-8, He said:“For many shall come in my name, saying, I am Christ; and shall deceive many. And ye shall hear of wars and rumours of wars… For nation shall rise against nation, and kingdom against kingdom: and there shall be famines, and pestilences, and earthquakes, in divers places. All these are the beginning of sorrows.”
Notice how Jesus spoke of deception (the Antichrist), wars (the sword), famine, and death—all aligning with the vision of the Four Horsemen. These signs remind us of the certainty of God’s Word and the urgency of living faithfully in preparation for His coming.
