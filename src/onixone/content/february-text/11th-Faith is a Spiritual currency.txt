The Parchments of Truth by Pastor Alex
11 February 2026
Bible: Hebrews 4
Key Verse: 2
2For unto us was the gospel preached, as well as unto them: but the word preached did not profit them, not being mixed with faith in them that heard it.
Theme:  Faith is a Spiritual currency
Message
As money is the currency of transaction in the marketplace of the world, so faith is the legitimate currency for transaction in the realm of the Spirit. It is the standard of life for every believer. Hebrews 10:38 declares: “Now the just shall live by faith; but if anyone draws back, my soul has no pleasure in him.” Faith is always in the “now,” for it is the standard of our daily walk with God.
Every child of God has been given a measure of faith. The growth of faith—from small to great—is your responsibility. You grow your faith through intentional study and meditation on the Word, choosing to believe and practice what God says. As you do this consistently, your faith will consequentially grow.
Faith means trusting God’s Word. Every word of God carries His vow; He has sworn that His Word shall come to pass (Isaiah 55:11). God is not a man that He should lie. Faith does not put you under pressure; that is unbelief. Faith is peaceful reliance on God with thanksgiving. Do your part, and let God do His part.
Unbelief is destructive; it spreads fear and discouragement like a cancer. Whatever God has spoken to you, stand upon it. And as you stand, be patient. Impatience leads to murmuring and complaining, which are the greatest barriers to the results of faith.
Prayer
Father, I trust in You. Your Word is the anchor of my soul, and every word You have spoken to me has its fulfilment. In Jesus’ name, Amen.
