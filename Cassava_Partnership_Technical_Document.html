Infrastructure & Integration Specifications

Project: Ecosystem Integration with Cassava Technologies
Prepared For: Cassava Technologies Technical & Engineering Team
Focus Areas: Integro OS, ProLens AI Studio, TalkWorld (TV & Media), OnixOne

1. The Ecosystem & What We Need From Cassava

We are building a connected suite of 4 major platforms. Here is what each platform does, and exactly what we need from Cassava's data centers to make them run flawlessly.

A. Integro OS & OnixOne (The Brain & Secure Enterprise Data)

What they do: Integro OS is the central brain that connects all our apps and manages user accounts. OnixOne is our heavy-duty document scanner that reads and processes complex corporate documents instantly.

What we need from Cassava:

  • Reliable Databases: We need rock-solid, managed databases to store all user data and keep all our apps synced in real-time.
  • High Processing Power (CPU/RAM): Reading and analyzing hundred-page corporate documents (OnixOne) requires servers with massive memory that can spin up exactly when a user uploads a file.
  • Strict Security (Data Privacy): Enterprise clients demand their files never leave the continent. We need Cassava's African data centers to guarantee local, encrypted data security.

B. ProLens AI Studio (Image & Video Generation)

What it does: ProLens is our professional AI design studio. Initially built for high-end photo editing, it is now expanding to include full AI Video Generation.

What we need from Cassava:

  • Top-Tier Graphics Processors (GPUs): Generating high-quality AI video and fixing photos requires extreme computing power. Standard servers cannot do this. We need dedicated access to Cassava's most powerful AI servers (like NVIDIA A100/H100s).
  • Speed (Low Latency): Our users expect the AI to work instantly. By hosting these heavy AI tasks on Cassava's African servers instead of routing to the US or Europe, we ensure the app feels lightning fast.

C. TalkWorld (Virtual Meetings, TV, and Streaming Media)

What it does: TalkWorld is much more than virtual meetings. It is a massive TV and Social Media Broadcasting Hub. Creators use it to generate content, host TV-style broadcasts, and stream video globally to massive audiences.

The ProLens & TalkWorld Connection: These platforms talk to each other. A creator can generate a stunning AI video inside ProLens, and with one click, send it to TalkWorld to be broadcasted on their streaming channel.

What we need from Cassava:

  • The Liquid Fiber Network: High-definition TV streaming and video calls cannot buffer or lag. We need maximum bandwidth and access to Cassava's fast Liquid Intelligent Technologies fiber network.
  • Massive Storage & Video Delivery: TV shows, podcasts, and video streams take up huge amounts of hard drive space. We need massive, affordable cloud storage lockers to hold all this media, plus a network designed to deliver these videos instantly to viewers' screens.

2. Summary: What We Are Asking For

To successfully run this ecosystem, we are asking Cassava for a strategic partnership that includes:

  1. Cloud Servers & Storage: Flexible server hosting and massive data storage space for our databases, apps, and TalkWorld TV media.
  2. AI Computing Power: Dedicated access to your highest-end AI processors (GPUs) to power ProLens Image and Video Generation.
  3. Fast Internet & Routing: Access to your top-tier network (Liquid Fiber) to guarantee lag-free TalkWorld broadcasts.

3. The Mutual Benefit: A Cutting-Edge Showcase

Cassava is building world-class infrastructure in Africa. We are building world-class software in Africa. By hosting Integro, TalkWorld, ProLens, and OnixOne, we will serve as a premier showcase of what Cassava's servers can handle. We will prove to the world that complex, AI-heavy, global TV platforms can be hosted and run flawlessly entirely on African soil.
